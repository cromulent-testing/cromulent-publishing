February 29 got you down?

Leap years are insidious. They show up every four years, just far enough apart that you forget about them. It sucks. So we wrote a book about them and other tricksy date and time things.

What's with the range?

Because of time zones there are 26 hours from when the first time zone enters a day and when the last time zone enters it. If you consider when the last time zone exists a day, there are 50 hours in every day.

That's covered in the book too, and a heap more.
